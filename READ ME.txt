BEST PLAYED IN CHROME.

OBJECTIVE - 

Destroy waves of enemies with your weapons.
Reach level 10.
Destroy the mothership and save Earth.
Acquire power ups that float on screen to aid you.


CONTROLS - 
 
W or UP ARROW    to move up
A or LEFT ARROW  to move left
S or DOWN ARROW  to move down
D or RIGHT ARROW to move right

SHIFT and D or
SHIFT and RIGHT ARROW to thruster boost right

SPACEBAR to fire laser
E or F   to fire torpedo
